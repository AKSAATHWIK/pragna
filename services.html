Family Counseling

Family counseling is a service available to anyone struggling to cope with family or relationship issues.The service is open to individuals, couples and families.Couples with young children and teenagers are given priority.Many people contact the family counseling service for advice about day-to-day problems,conflicts or crises in the family.We provide couples and family therapy, as well as individual counseling sessions.

Legal Notices

Legal notice is simply the requirement that a party must be furnished with sufficient knowledge concerning the legal processes that affect his rights and duties or obligations.A Legal notice in India is the very first step of any legal proceeding as it alerts a person to get prepared for a court action.A legal notice is like a word of warning sent as it lays down the conditions that the person receiving the notice is required to follow; otherwise a legal action may be taken against him.A person can easily describe his grievance in a legal notice with the help of an Advocate.
